# Deposit to <EnvText envKey="networkName"/>

Bridging assets to <EnvText envKey="networkName"/> takes less than **5 minutes**.

To bridge funds to <EnvText envKey="networkName"/> you can use our Bridge (<EnvLink envKey="bridgeDepositUrlMainnet">Mainnet</EnvLink> / <EnvLink envKey="bridgeDepositUrlTestnet">Testnet</EnvLink>) and follow these steps:

1. Connect your wallet ([Mainnet](../using_mainnet) / [Testnet](../using_testnet)) to the <EnvText envKey="networkName"/> Bridge.
2. Make sure you select the right asset that you want to bridge and that the **From** chain is <EnvText envKey="l1"/>.
3. Fill in the amount of <EnvText envKey="symbolMainnet"/> that you want to bridge. Ensure you have a positive balance of <EnvText envKey="symbolMainnet"/> on the <EnvText envKey="symbolMainnet"/> Chain.
4. Click on **Deposit** and wait for the transaction to go through.

<img src="/bridgein.png" width="500" />


When the transaction is successful, the transaction details can be seen by clicking on the **View recent transactions**.

<img src="/deposit_details.png" width="800" />

1. Click on the Txn Hash itself, it will jump to <EnvLink envKey="l1Explorer">transaction detials page</EnvLink>.
2. Click on **view**, it will jump to transaction status page below.

<img src="/deposit_progress.png" width="500" />

 Wait less than **5 minutes** to see the transaction show up with the status **Deposit finalized**.
