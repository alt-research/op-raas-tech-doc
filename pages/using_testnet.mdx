## Add <EnvText envKey="projectName"/> Testnet to MetaMask

### Using the <EnvText envKey="networkName"/> Rollup Info Page

1. Navigate to our <EnvLink envKey="rollupInfoPageTestnet">Rollup Info Page</EnvLink>
2. Click on the "**Add to Wallet**" button
